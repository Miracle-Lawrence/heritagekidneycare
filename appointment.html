Requesting an Appointment

We offer several convenient ways for you to schedule an appointment with our specialists at Heritage Kidney and Medical Care.

Call to Schedule an Appointment

You can request an appointment by calling our Front Desk on 09167760222 or 09167767081. Our staff will assist you in selecting a convenient date and time.

Send a Text Message

You may also request an appointment by sending a text message to our Front Desk on 09167760222 or 09167767081. A member of our team will respond to confirm your appointment.

Send a WhatsApp Message

For quick assistance, you can send a WhatsApp message to 09167760222 or 09167767081. Our team will help schedule your appointment and answer any questions you may have.

Visit the Hospital

You can also schedule an appointment by visiting the Front Desk at Heritage Kidney and Medical Care. Our staff will gladly assist you with booking a consultation with the appropriate specialist.

Request an Appointment Online

You can request an appointment online by filling out the appointment form below. Our Front Desk staff will contact you to confirm your appointment details.
