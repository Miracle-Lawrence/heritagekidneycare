Consultation

Routine Consultation

Heritage Kidney and Medical Care operates 24 hours a day to ensure continuous access to specialized renal services. Our medical and support teams are available around the clock, providing dedicated care and monitoring for patients at every stage of treatment.

Virtual consultations are available for patients who are unable to visit our locations in person. Patients can consult with our nephrologists and surgeons remotely to receive expert guidance, discuss treatment options, and plan for pre-operative evaluations and procedures before traveling.
