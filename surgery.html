Surgery

Kidney Transplant

At Heritage Kidney & Medical Care, our Kidney Transplant program provides life-saving solutions for patients with end-stage kidney disease. We combine advanced medical expertise with compassionate care.

Our Services Include:

  • Comprehensive pre-transplant evaluation
  • Living and deceased donor transplant programs
  • Expert transplant surgeons and nephrologists
  • Advanced surgical techniques
  • Post-transplant monitoring and care

Why Choose Us?

Our program improves quality of life by restoring kidney function and reducing dependence on dialysis, while ensuring long-term patient support.

Plastic Surgery

Our Plastic Surgery unit combines medical precision with aesthetic expertise to enhance appearance and confidence through personalized treatments.

Our Services Include:

  • Cosmetic procedures (facial, body contouring)
  • Reconstructive surgery
  • Non-surgical aesthetic treatments
  • Personalized consultations

Our Approach:

We deliver natural-looking results using modern, minimally invasive techniques while prioritizing patient safety and satisfaction.

General Surgery

Our General Surgery department provides expert care across a wide range of surgical conditions using advanced tools and experienced professionals.

Core Services:

  • Abdominal surgeries (appendix, hernia, gallbladder)
  • Minimally invasive (laparoscopic) procedures
  • Emergency surgical care
  • Pre- and post-operative management

Patient-Centered Care:

From diagnosis to recovery, we ensure safe procedures, accurate evaluation, and continuous monitoring for optimal healing.
