Patient Registration Notice

Please read the following carefully before completing your registration at Heritage Kidney & Medical Care:

  • Registration, Consultation, and Procedure Fees: All fees are non-refundable, even if referred to another specialist.
  • Referral to Specialists: See the General Duty Doctor first to avoid double consultation fees.
  • Electronic Medical Records & Prescriptions: All records are electronic. Prescriptions go directly to our Pharmacy; paper copies are not provided.
  • Laboratory Results: Lab results are posted electronically. Paper copies are available for a fee Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM – 9:00 PM.
  • Hospital Environment: Heritage Kidney & Medical Care is a no-smoking, alcohol-free, noise-free, and violence-free zone.
  • Payment of Fees: All services must be paid in full before being rendered.
  • Excuse Duty Notes: Papers can only be granted on the same day of the visit.

Please ensure you follow all guidelines to have a smooth registration experience.
