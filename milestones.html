Milestones & Achievements

Our Milestones & Achievements

Celebrating years of excellence in kidney care, transplants, and dialysis services.

Major Achievements

First Successful Kidney Transplant in Abuja

Performed in 2009, marking a historic milestone in renal healthcare in the Nigerian capital.

Largest Dialysis Facility in West Africa

With over 60 state-of-the-art dialysis machines, we provide care to a large patient base efficiently and safely.

Over 400 Successful Kidney Transplants

Maintaining an outstanding 98% success rate, reflecting our expertise and commitment to patient care.

Over 1,000 Dialysis Sessions Per Month

Delivering continuous life-saving care to patients with kidney failure every month.

Awards & Recognition

2025 Dove Globe Award

Recognized as the Best Dialysis Centre in Nigeria for excellence in patient care and innovation.

Award of Excellence by NANN

Honoring clinical excellence, dedication to patient care, and outstanding contributions to nephrology services.

Years of Service

Operating since 2009, Heritage Kidney & Medical Care has provided specialized renal care for over 14 years, continuously expanding its services and expertise across Nigeria and West Africa.
