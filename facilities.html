Explore Our Facilities

Reception

Our Reception serves as the first point of contact for all patients and visitors. Our friendly and well-trained staff are available to assist with patient registration, appointment scheduling, inquiries, and directions within the facility. We strive to provide a warm welcome and ensure that every patient receives prompt attention and guidance throughout their visit.

Pharmacy

The Pharmacy Unit at Heritage Kidney and Medical Care provides safe, reliable, and high-quality medications prescribed by our medical professionals. Our licensed pharmacists ensure that patients receive the correct medications along with proper guidance on dosage, usage, and possible side effects. We are committed to supporting patient recovery through accurate dispensing and professional pharmaceutical care.

Dialysis Unit

The Dialysis Unit is one of the core facilities at Heritage Kidney and Medical Care, dedicated to supporting patients with kidney-related conditions. Our unit is equipped with modern dialysis machines and operated by trained specialists who provide safe and comfortable treatment. We focus on delivering high-quality dialysis care while ensuring patient safety, dignity, and continuous monitoring throughout each session.

Laboratory

Our Laboratory is equipped to perform a wide range of diagnostic tests that support accurate medical evaluation and treatment. Using modern equipment and strict quality control procedures, our laboratory scientists deliver timely and reliable test results. These diagnostic services help our doctors make informed decisions for effective patient care.

Intensive Care Unit (ICU)

The Intensive Care Unit (ICU) provides advanced monitoring and specialized care for critically ill patients who require close medical supervision. Our ICU is equipped with modern life-support equipment and staffed by highly trained medical professionals who provide continuous care and rapid response to emergencies. Patient safety, stability, and recovery are our top priorities.

Advanced Surgical Theater

Our modern Operating Theater is designed for precision and safety, providing a sterile environment for a wide range of surgical procedures. Equipped with the latest surgical and monitoring technology, our theater ensures that every patient receives expert care. Highly trained surgical and support staff work together to maintain strict safety protocols, optimize outcomes, and support smooth recovery for all patients.
