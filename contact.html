Consultation & Patient Support

Routine & Specialist Consultation

At Heritage Kidney and Medical Care, we provide continuous access to specialized kidney care and medical consultation. Our experienced nephrologists and healthcare professionals are available around the clock to support patients with diagnosis, treatment planning, and long-term renal care.

We also offer virtual consultations for patients who are unable to visit our facility in person. Through remote consultation, patients can speak directly with our specialists, receive professional medical guidance, and prepare for treatments before arriving at our center.
